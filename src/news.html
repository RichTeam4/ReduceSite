Nov 12, 2018: New products available in the store!

I recently spent five days in the Canadian Rockies and made a few mistakes. The first mistake is not realizing how early the winter comes in the Rocky Mountains. I encountered snow on my first day of arrival. Unfortunately I didn’t bring enough appropriate clothing. So I had to hike in cold, wet and windy weather. But I still enjoyed so much. The glaciers, mountains, lakes, and meadows are the most gorgeous scenery you can find on the earth. I had planned to visit the Maligne Lake, but I didn’t realize the access road was closed till I got there. Considering the driving distance is 20 miles from my cabin, I basically wasted half a way. Another mistake I made is still weather related. The reason why I booked the cabin was because it was the most rustic among all. But I didn’t think enough about the fact that it only had a wood stove. It turned out I had to build a fire in the stove a couple times at night since it got very close to freezing point and the heater would go out in the middle of the night. Overall, it is a wonderful trip. I would plan better next time.

Redüce to Perform at Global Music Festival

For more than 20 years, musicians from all genres have gathered to benefit hunger in the underdeveloped areas of the world. The event has raised more than 10 million since the first concert in 1998. Jillian will be performing the songs from the new album. The concert will be held on March 8, 2019 at American Airline Center in Dallas TX.

TOUR DATES:
7/5 Chicago, IL – Chicago Theatre
7/10 Atlanta, GA – The Fox Theatre
7/15 Dallas, TX – American Airline Center
7/30 Austin, TX – One World Theatre
8/10 Oklahoma City, OK – Central National Bank Center
8/20 Las Vegas, NV – Aria Resort & Casino
8/31 Scottsdale, AZ – Scottsdale Center for the Performing Arts
9/10 Portland, OR – Paris Theatre
9/20 Riverside, CA – Fox Theatre

Tickets are on sale now.

Redüce Wins Country Music Award

Redüce received the Best Female Vocal Performance Award at the 2018 Country Music Awards.

This award was started in 1980 to honor extraordinary contributions to country music.

“I’m so proud of receiving this award for my music,” Jillian says. “I’m really excited to share my new album with everybody.”

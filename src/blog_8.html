Thinking of buying a house

Aug 08, 2018 Posted by Jillian In : Music

  • 12k
  • 11.2k
  • 1.8k

As of late, I’ve considered the life changing event of buying a house. I started to want to settle down in a place where my families and friends are. Also, I recently realized I pay the same amount of rent most people pay on their mortgage. It might be finally wise to purchase a house.

However, house shopping is a lot of work; I might not have time to do so. Moreover, owning a home is a huge commitment, which could be a lot more complicated than I expect. In addition, I am on the road half of the time, is renting still a better idea?
